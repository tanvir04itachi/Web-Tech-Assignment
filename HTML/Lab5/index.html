Published: March 14, 2025

After eight months of intense negotiations, representatives from 15 nations have signed a comprehensive trade agreement that aims to reduce tariffs and streamline cross-border commerce. The pact is expected to boost economic growth across the participating countries.

Global Tourism Shows Strong Recovery Post-Pandemic

Published: March 10, 2025

International tourism has rebounded to pre-pandemic levels according to the latest data from the World Tourism Organization. Popular destinations are reporting record numbers of visitors, signaling a robust recovery for the global travel industry.

The resurgence is attributed to pent-up demand, improved travel infrastructure, and new digital nomad visa programs in several countries. Industry experts predict continued growth through the remainder of the year.
